Discover the musical journey of singer-songwriter

ODETTE
QUESADA

Odette Quesada is a legendary Filipina singer-songwriter whose music has defined the sound of love and romance in Original Pilipino Music (OPM). From the very beginning of her career in the 1980s, Odette Quesada established herself as a prolific songwriter with an exceptional gift for weaving heartfelt lyrics into timeless melodies.

Odette Quesada first gained national attention when she won second prize in the 1982 Metro Manila Popular Music Festival (Metropop) amateur division for her song “Give Me a Chance,” performed by Ric Segreto. In 1983, Odette followed this with a monumental achievement—clinching the Grand Prize in the professional category at the 6th Metro Manila Popular Music Festival for “Till I Met You,” sung by Kuh Ledesma.
